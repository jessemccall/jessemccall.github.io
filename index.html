Personal Statement

I am in my fifth professional year currently working for Stantec in Brisbane since achieving a bachelors’ degree in Civil Engineering with honours at the University of Canterbury. My job title is “Civil Engineer” although my solidified experience spans through roading, water, and geotechnical areas. My passion lies most within the water and roading industries and these are the areas I aim to specialise in as my career develops.

My overarching personal work attributes are to always strive to deliver the best quality outputs, to work consistently hard every day, and to develop and learn with a holistic lens always. At this time of my career my focus is on developing expertise in the technical skills I’ve obtained, and learning/ progressing into a project manager of civil engineering projects. My ultimate career goal is to be exceptionally technically skilled in water infrastructure projects, as well as to be an exceptional personal and purpose-led team leader and project manager.
